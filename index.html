Sustainable Fashion: The Future of Style

Nov 12 2025

Artificial Intelligence is playing a growing role in the fashion industry, from generating new designs to enhancing customer shopping experiences. AI-driven design tools allow brands to experiment with creative styles that would be difficult to produce manually. Personalized shopping has also improved, with AI algorithms suggesting outfits based on user preferences. Additionally, virtual try-on features and augmented reality are making online fashion retail more interactive. However, some critics fear that AI could replace traditional fashion designers, raising concerns about the future of creativity in the industry.

Afrocritik: A Beacon for African Creativity

Afrocritik is a digital media platform that shines a spotlight on African art, entertainment, fashion, music, movies, and culture. Established in 2021 by Samson Jikeme and Owanate Max-Harry, it operates from Lagos and Los Angeles. The platform publishes insightful critiques and analyses, including end-of-year lists such as 'Top 50 African Albums' and 'Top 100 African Songs.' Additionally, Afrocritik hosts the Afrocritik Prize for Criticism, recognizing excellence in cultural commentary.
